Definitions of Time
  • Billable – Hours that TTEC bills clients for
  • Exception Time – Vacations, wellness, holidays, etc.
  • Working hours – Business hours that are not billable to external clients
Oracle Time & Labor

Use Oracle Time & Labor (OTL) for billable hours and exception time daily. Billable employees are hired into:

  • Cisco and Google Managed Services Roles
  • Cisco and Google Sales Engineers
  • Analytics
  • Consulting and IP Roles Globally (confirm with your manager, since systems vary based on project)
  • Public Sector Sales Team

Your manager will supply you with the project codes.
  • Go to Oracle T&L
  • Go to Oracle T&L training
Kronos – Hourly Employees

Employees who are paid on an hourly basis and eligible for overtime use Kronos for all working hours and exception time daily.
  • Go to Kronos
  • Go to Kronos Mosaic space
  • Go to Kronos training
Kronos – Support Salaried Employees

Support salaried employees, who are not eligible for overtime, use Kronos for exception time only. Do NOT enter working hours in Kronos. These employees are hired into:

  • Sales & Marketing (except Public Sector)
  • Sales Operations
  • Other support roles globally
Jira

Use Open Air for billable hours and exception time daily. Billable employees are hired into:

  • Amazon Connect
  • Amazon Web Services

Your Jira credentials, instructions, and login information arrive via email. Your manager will supply you with the project codes.
Open Air

  • Microsoft
  • Genesys
  • Project Management

You’ll receive your OpenAir login details and training during your first week.

Create a Travel Profile

All business travel must be booked using the Global Travel Desk (Concur). Contact the Global Travel Desk (traveldesk@ttec.com) to set up your profile. You’ll receive instructions with the next steps and links to follow. Normally, profiles are created automatically approximately 2-3 weeks from your date of hire. If you are trying to access Concur before your profile is automatically created, you may need help setting up your profile manually with a temporary password to book your business travel.

Once you have a travel profile, please visit the Travel Desk space on Mosaic for additional travel information, policies, and the latest updates and processes.
Apply for a Corporate Credit Card

Employees in countries outside the US, please confirm with your HCBP whether your country follows this process.

  1. Submit a ticket on ASK Now > Other Departments > Accounting > Global Procurement > Travel & Entertainment (T&C Card).

    Type of Request: Card Application

  2. In the comments section, indicate that you need to apply for a Corporate Credit Card.
  3. After creating the ticket, you’ll receive an email from a Corporate Credit Administrator with the forms you need to complete and detailed instructions. Please allow 48 hours for a response.
  4. Submit a second ticket on ASK Now > Other Departments > Accounting > Global Procurement > Travel & Entertainment (T&C Card).

    Type of Request: Card Application

  5. Attach to the second ticket the completed forms:
    • Application Form (to be attached to the ticket except for online application)
    • VP/SVP Approval
    • Corporate Card Agreement (CCA) Form

Feel free to review the Corporate Card space on Mosaic for more information and contact details.
Request Access to iExpense

iExpense is an Oracle-based tool that lets you submit business expenses for reimbursement.

To request access to iExpense, please follow these steps:

  1. Ensure that you have a company email address.
  2. Successfully complete the Oracle iExpense: Completing a Travel and Entertainment Expense Report course on TTEC Digital Talent.
  3. Submit a ticket on ASK Now > Information Technology > Applications and Software > Oracle Systems and Support Request.

    Service Catalog: iExpense

  4. Attach the certificate of completion that proves you completed the course.

Once you have access to iExpense, please submit your expenses by following the steps in this job aid.

Link Your Card to iExpense

Once your card arrives, link your card to iExpense in Oracle. To link your card to iExpense, submit a ticket on ASK Now > Other Departments > Accounting > Global Procurement > Travel & Entertainment (T&C Card). For the type of request, select the Card Linking option.

NOTE: You'll need to reference the last five digits on your card.
